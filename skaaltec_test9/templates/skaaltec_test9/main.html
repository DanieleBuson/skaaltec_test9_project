We are SKAALTEC




We are SKAALTEC






The company name “Skaaltec” combines a cheerful wish (in Scandinavian) for good health with the technology. This is exactly the mission of Skaaltec - to provide advanced neuroscience-inspired technology solutions for improved health in an accessible way for everyone.






Our Vision


Our journey begins with a profound realization - the existing landscape of stroke recovery is fraught with challenges and limitations. Conventional neural stimulation therapies have proven ineffective, invasive, exorbitant, or impractical, leaving patients and healthcare professionals yearning for a transformative solution. As a collective of pioneering neuroscientists and visionary engineers, we are driven by a mission to bridge the chasm that has long separated stroke survivors from their dreams of recovery. We have tapped into a network of clinical experts in brain physiology and neuroengineering, each of whom shares our passion for reshaping the future of rehabilitation.



Problems and Solution


Our quest unveils a striking void in the current approach to stroke recovery - the absence of a seamless integration between neural stimulation and the brain's own signals for motor learning and recovery. The missing link, we believe, is a conduit that unites these elements, propelling patients toward holistic healing. Furthermore, we've identified the scarcity of devices that not only expedite neural plasticity but also harness alternative motor control pathways crucial for regaining motor function. Traditional solutions often fall short, failing to initiate therapy until months or years after the initial stroke, a critical window of opportunity slipping through the cracks.





SmartVNS


SmartVNS is the embodiment of our vision. It emerges as a visionary leap forward in the realm of stroke neurorehabilitation and represents a paradigm shift, a breakthrough that promises to redefine the future of stroke neurorehabilitation. Our solution seamlessly marries neural stimulation with neurally-relevant movement-derived signals, offering a transformative approach to motor learning and recovery. We are pioneers in promoting long-term neural plasticity and activating essential motor control pathways, all within the early stages of recovery, harnessing the full potential of the brain's healing capabilities.
