Meet the team






Meet the talented team behind Skaaltec: Paulius Viskaitis, our visionary CEO; Dane Doonegan, our innovative CTO; and Olivier Lambercy, our skilled Rehabilitation Engineer. Together, they are driving our startup's success with their expertise and dedication.






Paulius Viskaitis

CEO

In the dynamic world of startups, the role of a CEO is undeniably pivotal, and Paulius Viskaitis's leadership at Skaaltec exemplifies this significance. As the captain of the ship, Paulius Viskaitis's vision, determination, and strategic acumen set the course for Skaaltec's journey. With unwavering commitment, Paulius Viskaitis fosters a culture of innovation, resilience, and adaptability, inspiring every member of the Skaaltec family to push the boundaries of what's possible.


Dane Doonegan

CTO

In the ever-evolving landscape of modern startups, the role of a CTO is equally vital, and Dane Doonegan's contribution to Skaaltec is nothing short of transformative. As the Chief Technology Officer, Dane Doonegan is the mastermind behind the technical architecture that powers Skaaltec's innovative solutions. His technical prowess and strategic thinking are the driving forces behind the company's product development and technological innovation. Dane Doonegan's leadership ensures that Skaaltec remains at the forefront of cutting-edge technology, enabling the startup to offer unique and competitive offerings to its customers.


Olivier Lambercy

Rehabilitation Engineer

In the realm of rehabilitation engineering, Oliver Lambercy plays an irreplaceable role in guiding and supervising the crucial work at Skaaltec. As the Rehabilitation Engineer overseeing the development of innovative solutions, Oliver Lambercy brings a wealth of expertise and a compassionate perspective to the team. His deep understanding of assistive technologies and his commitment to enhancing the lives of individuals with disabilities drive Skaaltec's mission to make a meaningful impact. His ability to collaborate with interdisciplinary teams and bridge the gap between technology and rehabilitation expertise is instrumental in delivering effective solutions.
